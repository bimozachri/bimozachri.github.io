Nike Air Jordan

Harga: Rp 499.000

The Jordan 1 Mid SE Ice Blue (2023) has a white midsole and an ice blue upper made of fine leather and synthetic materials. The laces and the outsole are also of an ice-blue hue. The shoe's perforated toe box and cushioned tongue provide ventilation and comfort. The outsole is made of tough rubber that offers stability and grip. The Nike Swoosh emblem appears in black on both sides of the shoe. The sneaker features an Air Jordan logo on one side. There is a Jumpman logo and "AIR" lettering on the tongue.
